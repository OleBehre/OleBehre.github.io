Welcome to Monnem-sur-Mer

You’ve been hired as the sole dispatcher for our coastal energy hub. It’s a simple job, mostly. Just keep the grid from exploding while our local polar bear watches you judgmentally.

  • The Goal: Keep the frequency at 50.00 Hz. If it drifts too far, the lights go out. Forever (or until you hit refresh).
  • The Assets: Toggle Solar, Wind, and Gas. Nuclear is too slow for your frantic clicking, so it stays on.
  • The Battery: The Auto-Balancer is your best friend—until it runs out of juice. Then you're on your own.
  • The Bear: He vibes when you're green. He stares when you burn gas. Don't make him sad.

Disclaimer: This is solely a fun private project. Real grid physics involve significantly more math and fewer dancing bears.
